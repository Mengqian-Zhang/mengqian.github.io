Mengqian Zhang

I am currently a Postdoctoral Associate at Department of Computer Science of Yale University, hosted by Prof. Fan Zhang.

Prior to this, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Stern School of Business of New York University, hosted by Prof. Xi Chen and Prof. Elynn Chen.

I got my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Shanghai Jiao Tong University where I was very fortunate to be advised by Prof. Xiaotie Deng and the B.E. degree in Computer Science from Ocean University of China in 2018.

I was a visiting scholar at Center on Frontiers of Computing Studies, Peking University from July 2019 to December 2022.

My research interests mainly include blockchain, decentralized finance, algorithmic game theory, FinTech, and multi-agent AI.
